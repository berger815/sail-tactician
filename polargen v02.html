WHY THESE DEFAULTS
A polar is the boat's target (best achievable) speed, not its average. Percentile estimators reject the slow samples from tacks, gusts handled badly, and chop, while still rejecting GPS spikes that a raw max would keep. Heel and turn-rate gating below removes maneuvering samples so only clean steady-state runs feed the table.
